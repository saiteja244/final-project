## 📖 Project Overview

**Editkaro.in** is a complete, responsive website developed for a social media marketing and video editing agency. The platform serves as an online portfolio and lead generation tool, showcasing the agency's video editing work across multiple categories while capturing potential client information through integrated forms.

### 🎯 Project Objectives

- Establish a professional online presence for the agency
- Showcase video editing portfolio across 9 different categories
- Generate leads through email subscriptions and contact forms
- Provide information about the agency's mission, vision, and team
- Offer a seamless user experience across all devices
